2Hollis Discography

Published on October 8, 2024

Musical Style

2Hollis is often recognized for a dark, atmospheric sound that merges distorted beats with haunting melodies. Their music typically carries an experimental edge, with layers of digital distortion and synthesized textures. Vocally, 2Hollis explores themes of alienation, self-discovery, and emotional depth, making the tracks introspective yet relatable to listeners who gravitate toward moody and futuristic soundscapes.

Discography Highlights

Debut EP/Single

This project introduced 2Hollis to the scene, showcasing a fusion of trap beats, electronic production, and vocal experimentation. It set the tone for the artist’s mysterious and avant-garde aesthetic.

Full-Length Album (2023) - Working Title: "Transcendence"

This release marked 2Hollis's deeper exploration into themes of identity, technology, and emotional vulnerability. The production is often dark, featuring heavy bass, industrial influences, and unique sound design elements.

Notable Tracks:

  • "Void" - A haunting exploration of isolation and inner turmoil, featuring layered synths and glitchy production.
  • "Hologram" - A more upbeat track that contrasts a catchy melody with introspective lyrics about living in a virtual or disconnected world.

Latest Release (2024) - Single: "Nocturnal Visions"

This single offers a more refined and polished sound, with cinematic production and atmospheric soundscapes. The lyrics reflect a journey through dreams, inner chaos, and the night’s psychological effects.

Noteworthy Features

Collaborations: 2Hollis has collaborated with several underground producers and artists in the electronic and trap scene, blending their distinct sound with the work of others to create unique tracks that push genre boundaries.

Remixes: Known for releasing remixes that completely transform mainstream hits into dark, electronic reimaginings with atmospheric depth.
